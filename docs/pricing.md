## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long after we talk do you start?**
A: Usually next day. Gold tier starts immediately after payment.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: During the discovery phase, yes. After we start development, cancellations are non-refundable.

**Q: What if I want to add something mid-project?**
A: Scope changes are documented and may extend timeline. Additional work charged hourly ($50-200/hr depending on complexity).

**Q: Do you offer discounts for multiple automations?**
A: Yes! Silver tier is a better deal than two Bronze tiers. Gold is unlimited, so multiple automations fit great.

**Q: Can you work with my existing scripts?**
A: Yes. If you have existing automation, we can:
- Improve and optimize it
- Integrate with new automations
- Refactor for better performance
- Update and document it

**Q: What if my Mac breaks?**
A: Your scripts are on GitHub, so you can reinstall on new Mac anytime.
