# Sci-Fi Movies - Essential Viewing

## Mind-Benders
1. **Coherence** (2013) - Dinner party meets quantum mechanics
2. **Primer** (2004) - Time travel that respects your intelligence
3. **Predestination** (2014) - Bootstrap paradox perfection
4. **The Man from Earth** (2007) - Just talking, but holy shit

## Modern Classics
1. **Ex Machina** (2014) - AI ethics wrapped in thriller
2. **Arrival** (2016) - Language as technology
3. **Blade Runner 2049** (2017) - How to do a sequel right
4. **Annihilation** (2018) - Cosmic horror meets biology

## Underrated Gems
1. **Moon** (2009) - Sam Rockwell one-man show
2. **Timecrimes** (2007) - Spanish time-loop masterpiece
3. **Under the Skin** (2013) - Alien perspective done right
4. **The Endless** (2017) - Lovecraft meets indie budget

## Action with Brains
1. **Edge of Tomorrow** (2014) - Groundhog Day meets alien invasion
2. **Dredd** (2012) - Confined space perfection
3. **Upgrade** (2018) - Body horror meets AI revenge
4. **District 9** (2009) - Apartheid allegory with aliens

## If You Want to Think for Days
1. **Stalker** (1979) - Tarkovsky's meditation on desire
2. **World on a Wire** (1973) - Simulation theory before Matrix
3. **Solaris** (1972) - Memory and grief in space
4. **Ghost in the Shell** (1995) - Identity in digital age

## Recent Must-Watch
1. **Everything Everywhere All at Once** (2022)
2. **Dune** (2021) - Finally done right
3. **The Platform** (2019) - Social commentary as horror
4. **I'm Your Man** (2021) - German AI romance that works
